Who We Are

Not just another travel agency

Bassa Travels is a boutique agency based in Spain, exclusively focused on Indonesia. We don't open group tours. We don't sell packaged itineraries. We design private experiences from scratch — crafted around each traveller's pace, taste, and purpose.

Our philosophy is simple: fewer clients, better trips. Every traveller who books with us receives personalised attention, carefully selected accommodation, and end-to-end support in Spanish — before, during, and after their journey.

We operate in the premium wellness segment: couples, honeymooners, families, and solo travellers who are ready to invest in a truly transformative experience in Indonesia.

Our traveller
profile

Understanding who we send helps you understand the quality of each partnership.

01
Demographics

Spanish-speaking adults, primarily from Spain and Latin America. Ages 30–60. High purchasing power, experienced travellers who have already "done" the usual destinations.

02
Travel Style

They seek privacy, calm, and authenticity. Slow itineraries. Quality over quantity. Willing to spend significantly for exceptional experiences that feel personal and curated.

03
Spend Profile

Premium segment. Mid-to-high-end villas and boutique hotels, private transfers, private guides, exclusive experiences. Average stays of 10–21 nights in Indonesia.

04
Motivation

Wellness, nature, culture and meaningful connection. They come to Indonesia intentionally — not by chance. They return home as advocates for the destination.
